The Story
Behind India Calling

India Calling was born from a deep love for India, travel, culture, and meaningful human connection.

Having lived across countries and experienced different cultures, I realized that the most memorable journeys are not just about places we visit, but about how those places make us feel. India, with all its richness, warmth, beauty, chaos, traditions, spirituality, and stories, has always had a way of touching people deeply.

Through India Calling, I wanted to create journeys that go beyond sightseeing — experiences that allow people to slow down, connect, discover, and truly feel the soul of India with comfort, authenticity, and heart.

Because sometimes, travel doesn't just change where we are… it reconnects us with who we are.

The People Behind India Calling

Founder & Host
Dee Sharma

Born and raised in India, I come from a background in Marketing and PR, having worked with corporates like Kingfisher and Aviva before moving to Singapore, where I spent 8 years working with an event-based company focused on creating meaningful experiences and connections.

Now based in the Philippines, my journey across countries, cultures, and people eventually led to the creation of India Calling.

My son Shivaay is the youngest member of India Calling and the heart behind many smiles along the way.

Co-Founder & Journey Partner
